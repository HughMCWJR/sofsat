About the People

Principal Investigator

Dr. Shubhra Kanti Karmaker ("Santu") is a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor in the department of Computer Science and Software Enginering at Auburn University, Alabama. His primary research interest lies at the intersection of Big Data, Artifical Intelligence and Natural Language Processing. Dr. Karmaker wrote the initial SOFSAT paper and was the Principal Investigator on this paper.

Graduate

Mousumi Akter is a MS + PhD student of the Computer Science and Software Engineering Department at Auburn University. She works with Dr. Karmaker in the Big Data Intelligence (BDI) Lab as a research assistant. Mousumi was the primary graduate student working on this paper.

Undergraduate

Will Rice is a senior undergraduate student at Auburn University, studying Software Engineering. He is a part-time Research Assistant for Dr. Karmaker and plans to graduate in Spring of 2021. Will was responsible for building this web demo that accompanies the paper.
