Lowering the cap on 401(k) contributions
Lawmakers are still considering changes to the amount of pretax income that workers can set aside in their 401(k) retirement accounts.
Yet they may have no choice: Senator Susan Collins, Republican of Maine, said this week that she opposed lowering the top tax rate for individuals earning more than $1 million, creating pressure for Republicans to include a higher bracket in order to win passage.
