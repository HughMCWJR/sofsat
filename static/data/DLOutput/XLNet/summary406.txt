Hillary Clinton New batch of Hillary Clinton emails obtained by legal group State department releases emails Democratic nominee failed to turn over, including interactions with lobbyists and donors as secretary of state
The emails are separate from a larger batch of several thousand work-related emails that FBI officials recovered from Hillary Clinton's private server.
The government found the newly disclosed messages during a search of agency computer files from longtime Clinton aide Huma Abedin.
Clinton campaign officials did not immediately answer questions about the issue.
