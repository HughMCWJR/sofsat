Donald Trump has talked big about the action he was going to take to combat the opioid epidemic, describing it as a "national emergency," which he said he was "saying officially, right now" back in August.
The day of the official declaration has come and ... Trump isn't declaring a national emergency.
Last week, he promised a "major statement" this week, leaving administration officials scrambling to figure out how they could possibly implement a national emergency on something as widespread and amorphous as the opioid epidemic.
