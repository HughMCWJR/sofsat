On Monday, the Senate will hold a procedural vote on a bill that would make abortion after 20 weeks illegal in every state in the country.
Advocates of abortion rights say 20-week bans at the state level have harmed women, forcing them to travel to another state, often at great expense, to get the care they seek.
The most comprehensive look at fetal pain to date was a literature review published in the Journal of the American Medical Association in 2005.
