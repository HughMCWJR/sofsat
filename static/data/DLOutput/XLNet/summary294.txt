In a stunning turn, Senate Republicans -- in the dead of night, on their last hope of passing any kind of Obamacare repeal bill -- came up short.
Three Republican senators -- Susan Collins of Maine, John McCain of Arizona, and Lisa Murkowski of Alaska -- voted to block the Senate's so-called "skinny" repeal bill.
In the past three days, enough Republicans refused to vote to roll back Obamacare's expanded health insurance coverage.
