It's no great credit to Attorney General Jeff Sessions that he finally recused himself from all Justice Department investigations relating to the 2016 presidential campaign -- and specifically from all current or future inquiries into Russian attempts to influence the election.
But until Thursday he refused to cede control over Justice Department investigations into contacts between the campaign and the Russian government.
