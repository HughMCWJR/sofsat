Immigration activists protesting the Trump administration's decision on the Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals program outside of Trump Tower in New York.
On Tuesday, a San Francisco federal district court judge enjoined--that is, blocked--Trump's attack on the Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals, or DACA, program.
The good news is by granting a preliminary injunction the judge has indicated that he thinks DACA's backers are likely to prevail.
