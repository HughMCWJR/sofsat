On Tuesday night, President Donald Trump will deliver an address to a joint session of Congress in Washington, the biggest speech he's given since his inaugural address back on Jan. 20.
That speech is used to reflect on the progress of the previous year and help set the president's agenda for the coming year.
Still, it's been tradition in recent administrations for the new president to deliver a speech to a joint session to Congress in the early weeks or months of his term.
