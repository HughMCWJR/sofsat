In a stunning turn, Senate Republicans -- in the dead of night, on their last hope of passing any kind of Obamacare repeal bill -- came up short.
Diagnosed with brain cancer and the subject of much speculation after a rousing Senate speech calling for a return to an open and bipartisan process in the Senate, the Arizona Republican joined the other Republican dissenters at the climax of the Senate health care fight -- and helped kill the bill.
But in the course of a week, Senate leadership came up with three different plans to try to achieve the goal of repealing Obamacare, and they fell short every time.
