According to the office of the mayor of London, in the six days after the terrorist attack at London Bridge and Borough Market on June 3, the Metropolitan Police reported 120 Islamophobic events, compared with 36 the previous week.
On Monday, the Muslim Association of Britain condemned the assault in Finsbury Park as an "evil terror attack."
The association also called for the police to protect mosques and asked the government to fight hate crimes against Muslims.
