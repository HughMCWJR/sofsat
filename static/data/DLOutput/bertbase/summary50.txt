Party-line votes by the House Energy and Commerce and Ways and Means Committees sent the measure to the House Budget Committee for consideration next week before a final House vote that Speaker Paul D. Ryan plans for later this month.
When House Republicans finally unveiled the legislation Monday night, he declared on Twitter, "Our wonderful new Healthcare Bill is now out for review and negotiation" -- hardly a hard line.
"Today marks the beginning of the end of Obamacare," Representative Steve Scalise of Louisiana, the majority whip, declared after the votes.
