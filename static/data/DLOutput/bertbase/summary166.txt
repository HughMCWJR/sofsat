US immigration Trump to end Daca 'Dreamers' program with six-month delay - reports President will end Obama program giving work permits to illegal immigrants, sources say, but Congress will now be given time to draft replacement law What is Daca and who are the Dreamers?
The Obama administration policy protects nearly 800,000 young men and women often called "Dreamers" from deportation and allows them to work legally.
The decision to give Congress half a year to come up with an alternative represents a compromise after Republicans and business leaders asked Trump to keep the program.
