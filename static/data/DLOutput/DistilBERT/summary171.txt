Mitt Romney, the former governor of Massachusetts, is now a Senate candidate in Utah -- and the announcement of his candidacy shows he's sensitive about being labeled a carpetbagger.
"Utah," the Republican says at the beginning of a video he shared Friday morning on social media, "is admired not only for its beauty, but also for the character of its people.
One reporter joked on Twitter that he lost count of how many times Romney said "Utah."
