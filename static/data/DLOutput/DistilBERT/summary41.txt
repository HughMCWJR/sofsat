With Trump running as the nominee for president, the GOP had steeled themselves for at least four more years of sabotage against Hillary Clinton.
Republicans had voted to repeal the ACA over 60 times since it was passed.
It stretches the imagination to think that the coordination needed for 60 votes to repeal Obamacare by the GOP could not produce a coherent bill to replace the landmark law when finally given the chance, but to nobody's surprise these 60 votes were never meant to repeal anything.
