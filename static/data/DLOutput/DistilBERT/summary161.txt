US Congress Congress agrees to raise US debt ceiling and provide $15bn in hurricane aid Bill passed House 316-90 in deal struck between Trump and Democrats Legislation will fund federal government through 8 December The deal's genesis was an Oval Office meeting on Wednesday between Trump, Steve Mnuchin, Mike Pence and congressional leaders from both parties.
The deal, which was approved by the Senate on Thursday by 80-17, came as Hurricane Irma bore down on Florida.
All 90 votes against came from Republicans.
