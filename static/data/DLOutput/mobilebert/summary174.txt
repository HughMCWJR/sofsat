Last year, Trump famously proposed eliminating the National Endowment for the Arts and National Endowment for the Humanities and privatizing the Corporation for Public Broadcasting--which, in part, funds PBS.
But those ideas never actually materialized.
In this year's budget proposal, though, Trump seems to be taking a second stab at killing Big Bird's erstwhile home by once more suggesting that the government yank funding for the N.E.A.
