Starting with Comey's sudden firing on May 9, events have been unfolding since then, with near-daily scoops about the interactions between Trump and Comey and the campaigns connections to Russia -- it has been a wild ride up until this point.
Most of what we knew before Thursday has come in bits and pieces.
Journalists outside the White House (and the public) have struggled to put together scraps from anonymous sources, often only revealing a little information at a time.
