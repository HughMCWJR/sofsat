NEW: Trump administration will put its travel ban on refugees and migrants from 6 countries into effect at 8 p.m.
ET tomorrow.
By Arshad Mohammed and Yeganeh Torbati WASHINGTON (Reuters) - Visa applicants from six Muslim-majority countries must have a close U.S. family relationship or formal ties to a U.S. entity to be admitted to the United States under guidance distributed by the U.S. State Department on Wednesday.
