The Navy released the names on Sunday night of the seven sailors who were killed.
The victims were all located in flooded berths, the Navy said.
They were identified as Dakota Kyle Rigsby, 19, of Palmyra, Va.; Shingo Alexander Douglass, 25, of San Diego; Ngoc T. Truong Huynh, 25, of Oakville, Conn.; Noe Hernandez, 26, of Weslaco, Tex.
