Tensions over government spending have run high in recent weeks, increasing the risk of a shutdown.
On Thursday, Congress passed yet another stopgap government funding measure -- a continuing resolution -- keeping spending levels at status quo until January 19 to buy Congress more time to hash out a deal.
Democrats have an opportunity to push some of their legislative priorities, like passing protections for undocumented immigrants in the Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA) program, which President Donald Trump is ending, and disaster relief funding.
